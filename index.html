How to Play

Welcome to Wizard's Space Adventure! Here's how you can control Alaric and his spaceship:

  • Move: Use W, A, S, D or the arrow keys to move your spaceship.
  • Shoot: Point and click with your mouse to shoot at enemies.
  • Phase Shift: Press E to phase shift. This will change your color, making you immune to enemies of the same color.
